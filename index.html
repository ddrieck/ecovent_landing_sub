The Story

Most home heating and cooling systems operate like a home with only one light switch – the entire home is either on or off. Either every room is conditioned or none are. With this outdated design, it’s no wonder heating and cooling make up nearly half of home energy usage. Every room of your home is a little bit different, but even the smartest thermostat thinks they’re all the same. That means some rooms are freezing cold while others are uncomfortably hot.

ecoVent is an MIT developed, easy to install, wireless system of vents and sensors that makes your home comfortable and efficient by automatically redirect air to the rooms that need it most. It offers unparalleled control and saves a ton of money by putting an end to conditioning empty rooms.
